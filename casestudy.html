The Center Orlando

The Problem

The Center provides a vast amount of information! However, the lack of a visual & information hierarchy, overloads & hinders the user from utilizing all the resources The Center has to offer.

The Solution

By reorganizing the navigation & homepage as well as implementing effective design principles, we can help users access resources efficiently using 3 clicks or less.

Getting to know the users

There are many different users for this site - LGBTQ+ community members, allies, teens, parents, volunteers & supporters. I reached out to the LGBTQ+ Community to conduct 1:1 interviews & surveys to get to know their goals, likes, pain points & needs.
